Abdunazarov Akbarjon

Uzbek scientist, teacher, seismologist, and researcher specializing in seismic engineering, earthquake-resistant structures, and civil engineering.

About

Abdunazarov Akbarjon is an Uzbek scientist and researcher working in the field of seismic engineering and civil engineering. His professional interests focus on earthquake-resistant structures, seismic safety of buildings, soil–structure interaction, and innovative methods for mitigating seismic surface waves.

He is actively involved in scientific research related to seismic protection systems, structural safety, and applied engineering solutions aimed at improving the resilience of buildings and infrastructure in seismic regions.

Research Interests

  • Seismic engineering and earthquake-resistant design
  • Seismic safety of buildings and structures
  • Soil–structure interaction
  • Seismic surface wave attenuation methods
  • Civil and structural engineering

Academic and Professional Activity

Abdunazarov Akbarjon is engaged in academic and educational activities, combining teaching with scientific research. His work includes participation in research projects, preparation of scientific publications, and involvement in conferences related to civil engineering and seismic safety.
