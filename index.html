Abdunazarov Akbarjon

Uzbek scientist, teacher, seismologist, and researcher specializing in seismic engineering, earthquake-resistant structures, and civil engineering.

About

Abdunazarov Akbarjon is an Uzbek scientist and researcher working in the field of seismic engineering and civil engineering. His professional interests focus on earthquake-resistant structures, seismic safety of buildings, soil–structure interaction, and innovative methods for mitigating seismic surface waves.

Research Interests

  • Seismic engineering
  • Earthquake-resistant structures
  • Seismic safety of buildings and infrastructure
  • Soil–structure interaction
  • Seismic surface wave mitigation
